The Section 1 Project

Section1 is a movement. It is an exercise. A celebration. An example of how the creative minds of our generation can shape communities and enrich our lives. Our foundation is dedicated to transforming, vacant, underutilized, or derelict sites into vibrant creative spaces designated for the use of urban art. To do this we use inclusive, democratic and engaging processes aimed at fostering opportunities that promote and produce vibrant and progressive forms of creative expression.

Our parks, designated as Section1 Parks, will be developed through the practice of creative placemaking. They will focus on arts and creativity, leverage from the creative potential of its direct community, celebrate diversity, encourage citizenship, and serve as an agent for both social and economic prosperity.

Our current project is aimed at transforming a 3-acre site located behind Maryland Institute College of Art and directly underneath the Jones Falls Expressway into the worldâ€™s largest urban art park. The locality and layout of this site currently provide the opportunity for an 18,000 SF skate park, over 60,000 SF of paintable surfaces, several live performance spaces, and an acre of green space. The completion of this site will provide Baltimore with a premier urban arts venue to showcase and celebrate the amazing and cultural diverse pool talent in our city.
